About US

More Than Just Coffee

Bellavelle Café is a vintage-inspired girls-only café designed to offer comfort,
elegance, and warmth. With its soft floral décor, golden mirrors, velvet seating, and cozy atmosphere,
Bellavelle creates a charming space where girls can relax, connect, and enjoy quality time together. More than just a café,
it is a peaceful retreat filled with beauty, delicious drinks, and unforgettable moments.
